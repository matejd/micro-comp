Comparison of Microfacet D and G terms

Point illumination only! Equations for various terms can be found in many places, e.g. [1].

Differences are subtle, but here are some things you might notice:

  • Beckmann D is almost indistinguishable from Blinn-Phong.
  • Trowbridge-Reitz D has "softer" highlights (longer tails) than Blinn-Phong.
  • Geometric shadowing term G mostly affects glancing angles (rotate the camera to face the light source).
  • Implicit G is "darker" than others.
